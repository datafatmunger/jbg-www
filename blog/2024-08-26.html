1st day of classes (en_US)

I met with my advisor, Dr. Fan Chen, and brought the following list of items I find interesting:

  • CryptoQFL, FHE, QHE
  • Clocks, discrete, quantized, time
  • Cluster Simulation (MBQC)
  • Error Correction (QECC)
  • GPU Quantum Simulation
  • Analog Computer Quantum Simulation (leveraging for example Mythic.ai)

She suggested these were not 1 to 1.5 year topics, but basically 5+ year topics, or PhD topics, and suggested I start research building on work already happening in her group, that said she was open to the idea I start a bigger topic, with the contingency it will be longer to my first publication.

She suggested the following papers to start with:

  • IQGAN: Robust Quantum Generative Adversarial Network for Image Synthesis On NISQ Devices
  • QuanTA: Efficient High-Rank Fine-Tuning of LLMs with Quantum-Informed Tensor Adaptation
  • QDoor: Exploiting Approximate Synthesis for Backdoor Attacks in Quantum Neural Networks
  • Realization of Real-Time Fault-Tolerant Quantum Error Correction
  • Digital zero noise extrapolation for quantum error mitigation

I'll be digesting these papers over the next few days.
